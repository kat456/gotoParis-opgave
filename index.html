The city of love

Paris is beautiful both where it’s grand, like in the city’s heart near the Eiffel Tower, and where it’s intimate, like along Montmartre’s cobblestoned streets.

The skyline is protected by UNESCO and buildings can’t be more than six stories tall, so there’s no part of the city with an obscured view.

Paris is relatively small and the streets are extra-wide, making it the perfect city for strolling hand-in-hand.

Hôtel Alfred Sommier

Built in 1860, the private Parisian house of Alfred Sommier, a prominent French sugar industrialist, was just restored and transformed into a luxury hotel. Only a 100m from Place de la Madeleine, this private hotel has kept the soul of the Sommier family home.

It offers 80 rooms including 20 suites, as well as a restaurant and a bar overlooking a private garden. This historic building has retained its original style, while incorporating modern artistic creations into each rooms’s décor ensuring timeless French elegance.

The atmosphere of Alfred Sommier’s former social functions and professional receptions are still present in the meeting rooms and dining rooms.

You will be able to personally meet the heir of the Sommier family who is at the origin of the hotel.

The garden and terraces, independent of the main courtyard, invite you to relax and share a good homemade meal.

Hôtel California Paris Champs-Élysées

Built in 1925, the Hotel California Paris Champs Elysées, an Haussmanian style building, has been the favorite luxury hotel of American Press and Hollywood Stars. More than just a hotel, this is an authentic art gallery for art lovers with its private collection of over 3000 oil paintings. With its fine sense of luxury, this 4 star hotel has been turned into a family mansion just a step away from the Champs Elysées.

The Hotel California will offer 172 rooms and Suites each tastefully designed. You can relax in our Lounge Bar or in our Interior Patio and taste our signature cocktails and wide wine offer. The menu tailored by our Chef Gilles Courtin offers a wide selection of seasonal dishes.

You will enjoy our full American buffet breakfast every day at the restaurant « Le Cadre d’Or ». The Hotel California Paris Champs Elysees is also the perfect place to meet, with 7 fully equipped conference rooms which makes it the ideal venue for businessmen to hold their meetings from 10 to 110 people.

Hôtel de Buci

The Hotel Buci is a charming 4-star Parisian hotel in the Rive Gauche district, at the very heart of Saint-Germain-des-Prés, situated very close to the Louvre, the Sorbonne (in the Latin Quarter), the Musée d'Orsay and the Jardin du Luxembourg (the Senate).

Whether you prefer contemporary Art, primitive Art or modern Art, the hotel's ideal position will allow you to enjoy the many art galleries found between the Pont des Arts and the Jardin du Luxembourg, and particularly on Rue de Seine. There are also a number of designer shops nearby, such as Armani, Hugo Boss, Yves Saint Laurent and Le Bon Marché. Facing a typical Parisian market, this luxurious hotel with all the charm of French style is the perfect place for you to enjoy the unique atmosphere of Saint-Germain-des-Prés and the Rive Gauche district.

Soundproof air conditioned rooms looking, tastefully decorated with original engravings and new editions of warm-colored 18th century fabrics, different on each floor. Equipped with blinding electric shutters, a minibar, TV, a direct telephone line, a WiFi connection and an individual marble bathroom.
